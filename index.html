Register Rules

Rules


  1. Preliminaries – Online and Offline

    1. There are 2 modes of entry for both Corporate and Student teams:
      2. Mode 1: Online Prelims 2018 (November 24)
      Mode 2: Offline prelims 2018 (December 3)
    2. Each team will consist of two participants. The participants should be from the same organization/ institution.Cross teams will strictly not be permitted.
    3. Team composition cannot be changed after the preliminary rounds
    4. Teams will have to register to participate for both online and offline preliminaries.
    5. A minimal registration fee will be charged for teams participating in the offline prelims. The fees would be Rs.600/ team
    6. Each team is allowed to participate in one or all the two online prelims with the understanding that the best score will be considered for the screening. The 3 online prelims are independent of each other and aggregate scores will not be considered.
    7. In each round of the online prelims, only one submission per team will be allowed. Multiple logins, entries and submissions will lead to disqualification of the team.
    8. Teams which have not qualified in any of the Online Prelims can make another attempt by attending the Offline Prelims to be held in the FISAT Business School Campus on December 3rd. This will be the final mode of entry into the Quarter-Finals.
    9. Teams qualifying in the online prelims and offline Prelims will compete in the Quarter-Finals to be held on December 3rd at the FISAT Business School Campus.
  2. Quarter-Finals
    1. Quarter-final rounds for Online and offline students will be held separately.
    2. A total of 16 teams will enter unto quarter finals which includes Eight teams selected through the two online prelims and eight teams from the offline prelim
    3. 4 winning from each category (Online & offline) would be moving to the semi-finals.
  3. Semi-Finals
    1. 8 Winning teams from the quarterfinal would be competing with each other in this round.
    2. 6 best learns would be moving to the grand finale of BRAINWAVE 2018.
  4. Grand Finale
    1. The Grand Finale will feature 6 top teams from each category.
    2. The winning team will win Rs. 75,000/- as cash prize.
    3. The Runner Up team in will win Rs. 35,000/- as cash prize.
Note:

In case of an unforeseen emergency that results in the inability of a team which has qualified from the online prelim to participate, the following rules shall apply:

  1. If the FORUM BRAINWAVE 2018 Organizing team is informed of the qualifying team’s inability to be present for the quarter-finals on December 3rd, 2018 at least 3 days before the event or prior, the next team with the best qualifying score from the online preliminaries will be invited. Such successive invitations will continue till team number 10 on the list. If the 10th team on the Top Ten scorers list of the online preliminaries also declines participation due to valid reasons, then the slot shall be opened to accommodate a team from the offline preliminary round that will be conducted on the campus.
  2. If the FORUM BRAINWAVE 2018 Organizing team is not informed of the qualifying team’s inability to be present for the quarter-finals on December 3rd, 2018 at least 3 days before the event, the vacant slot will be opened to accommodate a team from the offline preliminary round that will be conducted on the campus.
